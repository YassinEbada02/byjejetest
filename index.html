Our Heritage

Engineered Chic

We believe phone cases are a personal fashion statement. Our mission is to combine shockproof engineering with soft, gorgeous palettes and individual artistic expression.

Luxury Craftsmanship

Founded in Cairo, Egypt, By jeje. was born out of a desire for artistic, soft-palette phone bumper overlays. By marrying protective clear bumper composites with beautiful holographic prints, wood textures, and monogram prints, we deliver pieces that spark joy.

Every case is impact tested up to 10 feet. We use durable materials and dual-coat scratch-proof layers to keep your phone looking brand new, regardless of drops or outdoor element exposure.
